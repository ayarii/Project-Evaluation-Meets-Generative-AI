Evaluator reference

AI usage scale (N1 → N5)

Inspired by the AI Assessment Scale, each level must be supported by observable behaviors — not just a student declaration.

N1

No AI

Work based on documentation, human resources, and personal reflection.

N2

Preparation with AI

Ideation, research, brainstorming — then rewritten in the student's own words.

N3

Collaboration with AI

Assistant for code or text, with systematic review and corrections.

N4

Full integration

AI orchestrated at every stage, with model choices and limit assessment.

N5

Creative exploration

Innovative, reflective use with bias questioning and documented experiments.
